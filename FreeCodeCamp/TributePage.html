Linus Torvalds

Creator of Linux and Git

"I care about the technology and the kernel - that's what's important to me."

Here's a short time line of Linus Torvalds' life:

  • 28 December 1969 - born in Helsinki, Finlsnd
  • 1987 - bought his first computer, a Sinclair QL
  • 1988 - enrolled in the University of Helsinki
  • 17 September 1991 - a crude version (0.01) of new operating system completed
  • 5 October 1991 - version 0.02, the first official version announced
  • 1997 - moved to Santa Clara, California to accept a position with Transmeta Corporation
  • 1999 - he was offered stock options by Red Hat and VA Linux. The stock prices increased to about $20 million after the companies went public
  • 2000 - worked for Open Source Development Lab (OSDL) based in Beaverton, Oregon
  • 2005 - developed 'Git'
